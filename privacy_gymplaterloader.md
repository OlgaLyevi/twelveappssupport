# Privacy Policy

**Gym Plate Loader** respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
Gym Plate Loader does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or precise location.

## Analytics & Usage Data
Gym Plate Loader uses **Google Analytics for Firebase** to collect anonymized usage data to help improve the app.

This may include:
- App interactions (such as screens viewed and features used)
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- General location information (country or region)
- App performance and usage statistics

This data is used solely for app analytics and improvement and is not used to identify individual users.

## Advertising
The app may display ads. Advertising services may use device identifiers to serve ads.
Gym Plate Loader does not directly collect or store personal data for advertising purposes.

## In-App Purchases
Optional in-app purchases may be offered. All payments are processed securely by Apple.
Gym Plate Loader does not have access to your payment or billing information.

## Children’s Privacy
Gym Plate Loader does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

## Data Sharing
Analytics data is processed by **Google** in accordance with their privacy policy:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Gym Plate Loader does not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Your Choices
You can limit analytics collection through your device’s privacy settings.
