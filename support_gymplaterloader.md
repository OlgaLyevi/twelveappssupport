## About the app
Gym Plate Loader is a simple barbell plate calculator designed to help you load the bar correctly in seconds.

---

## Common questions

### How are plates calculated?
Plates are calculated per side based on your selected bar weight and the available plate sizes.

### What does “Round to available plates” mean?
When enabled, the app rounds down to the nearest weight that can be loaded using available plates.

### Does the app work offline?
Yes. Gym Plate Loader works completely offline.
